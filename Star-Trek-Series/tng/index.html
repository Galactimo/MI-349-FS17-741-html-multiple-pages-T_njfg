Star Trek The Next Generation

Star Trek Deep Space 9 was a series that ran from 1987 to 1994 and followed directly in the footsteps of the original series just set around 70 years in the future from the last voyage of the orignal enterprise crew. The enterprise in this series is a new Galaxy Class ship that is dubbed the Enterprise-D. This series follows a whole new crew on the same mission to boldly go where no one has gone before!
