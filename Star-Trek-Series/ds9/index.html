Star Trek Deep Space 9

Star Trek Deep Space 9 was a series that ran from 1993 to 1999 and broke from the normal Star Trek formula of being set in a ship that was exploring new territories throughout space. This series took place on a space station that orbited an alien planet that had just broken a hostile occupation from a different hostile race. The Federation of Planets is helping this planet (Bajor) rebuild and helping maintain access to the first stable wormhole from the Deep Space 9 Space Station.
